Exchange /Refund Policy
Welcome to the TOYO Toys website. This exchange policy will explain the terms and conditions of exchanging your products purchased from us.
Exchange Policy
You may return or exchange products for up to 30 days from the date of purchase. Products should be returned in the original condition and packaging, without any visible damage, writing, or other alterations.
We do not cover shipping fees for the return of exchanged items. The shipping fee is non-refundable and should be paid by the customer.
Once your exchange has been received and inspected, we will notify you via email that your exchange has been processed and the shipping label for your replacement item has been generated.
We only offer exchanges on defective items or due to a change in mind.
Refund Policy
If you are not satisfied with your purchase for any reason, you may return the product in its original condition and packaging, within 30 days of purchase. Products that have been opened or used cannot be returned, except if it is defective.
Once your refund request has been received and inspected, we will notify you via email that your refund has been approved or declined. If approved, the refund amount will be credited to your original payment method, typically within 7-10 business days.
If the returned product is found to be in a non-defective condition, we may choose to withhold a refund and instead offer a store credit.
